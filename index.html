Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling with Propel

How does your pricing algorithm work?

Our AI analyzes 50+ data points including recent sales, neighborhood trends, property condition, sunlight exposure, and market velocity to determine the optimal listing price that maximizes both speed and value.

What fees do you charge?

We charge a transparent 1.5% commission fee—significantly lower than traditional agents. No hidden marketing costs, no surprise fees. What you see is what you pay.

How long does the selling process take?

On average, properties receive offers within 14 days and close within 30-45 days. Our fastest sale closed in 8 days from listing to offer acceptance.

Do you provide legal and financial support?

Yes. We work with a verified network of solicitors, financial advisors, and tax specialists to ensure smooth transactions. Support is included in our service.
